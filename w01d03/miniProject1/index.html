About Me

I am 24 years old. I am currently training to become an SDET, with Sparta Global. This is my personal blog site that follows my ethos there is no third step.

There is no Third step is a simple ethos that came about after my brother tried to install a rail on my grandad's bed with the aid of my grandmother.

The installation was simple.

Step one

You took the straps and pulled them under the bed.

Step 2

Attatch the straps together and tighten to secure.

I walked in after hearing the shouting, and heard my brother explain this and suddenly start screaming "There is no third step". After laughing about it with my family later on i thought about that phrase and how it could relate to how we work as people and my own lack of motivation and depression at the time.

So to finish my rambling explanation, this quite simply came the basis of my new way of thinking, rather than planning things in exhaustive lists that depress you.

Start with step one, decide to do something. Then go to step 2, Do it.
